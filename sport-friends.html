My Sport Friends -

Active Sports, Location & Availability

With Sport Social you can easily invite your friends to join and add people to your Sport Friends page that you have connected or played with. It’s easy to see who is active, + available and message them in the app.

We all have friends that we play different sports at different times. Now we can see when friends want and have the time. All you have to do is send them an open invite or message on our app. You can also send them a calendar invite when you’ve confirmed your time and place!
